Indigenous Populations in Canada

An Analysis of Population Distribution and Health Outcomes (2021 Census Data)

1. Population Distribution by Indigenous Group

This visualization shows the distribution of Indigenous populations across First Nations, Inuit, and Métis groups in Canada.

2. Geographic Distribution of Indigenous Populations

This interactive map displays the percentage of Indigenous populations and counts for First Nations, Inuit, and Métis groups across Canadian provinces and territories. Use the layer control to switch between different views.

3. Mental Health and Food Insecurity Analysis

This scatter plot explores the relationship between self-reported poor to fair mental health and food insecurity among Indigenous and non-Indigenous populations. Use the animation controls to view data by sex.
